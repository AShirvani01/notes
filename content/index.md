# Hey 👋

I'm Abtin. Just a UofT Stats/Bio undergrad. And the dog in the box is Ara.

## Why make this site?

My main goal is to document what I've learnt over the years pertaining to Stats/ML and ultimately solidify my understanding of key concepts. This is an ongoing, long-term project for myself and anyone who wants to take a look (although I don't think anyone will look at this😅). You can check out my [[To-do list]] for topics I plan to cover. Below are a few mini-goals I focus on when writing...

### Explain simply
Even simple concepts can be made confusing by excessive technical jargon. While I understand it's necessary for rigor, adding big-picture summaries and motivation/intuition for what we're doing can't hurt. I'd also like to add coding examples where applicable.

### Standardize notation
Every professor, textbook, and domain have slightly different ways of expressing the same ideas. For learners, this can be a bit of a headache, so I want to address these inconsistencies by standardizing my own notation.

### Bridge concepts
Most concepts are related in some way, which makes creating a linear curriculum rather challenging. In an academic setting where topics must be divided into courses, it can sometimes be difficult to see these connections. I want to bridge this gap using a tree-based approach to note-taking, allowing for a more natural exploration of topics.
